Initially, bearing the stage name Sphinx, He would later change it to Mic High Priest and eventually shorten it to MHP. While studying for an Electrical/Electronics Engineering degree in Nigeria, he met people who would become life long musical colleagues. They formed a Hip Hop group using the name SoulJazz and recorded over 100 songs collectively. This collective included names like FirstBorn Soulomon (real name Emem Akpan), Rapsom' (real name Atta Anago), G.A.B (real name Gabriel Akpabio), Aqbar (real name Ellis Ajoku), Swarthy Soul (real name Walter Sule), RhymeMastaRichy (real name Richard Eton) and MHP (real name Samuel Abu). An album was released in 2010 from selected songs.

In 2013, He moved to South Africa. He went back to University and graduated with a Computer Science degree at the University of the Western Cape in Cape Town, South Africa. Still dabbling into music once in a while, MHP has continued to stay in South Africa.
